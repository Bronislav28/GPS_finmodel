GPS Finmodel Report (2026–2030)

Ключевые формулы:
  • Workplace annual tokens = active_users × tokens_per_active_user_per_day × working_days_per_year
  • Contact Center annual tokens = interactions_per_day × automation_rate × tokens_per_interaction × calendar_days_per_year
  • weighted_throughput = 1 / Σ(model_share / throughput_per_gpu)
  • tokens_per_second = total_annual_tokens / (working_days_per_year × working_hours_per_day × 3600)
  • required_gpu = ceil(tokens_per_second / (weighted_throughput × utilization) × peak_factor)
  • required_gpu_increment = max(required_gpu_t − required_gpu_(t−1), 0), для первого года = required_gpu
  • gpu_capex = required_gpu_increment × gpu.unit_cost
  • total_capex = gpu_capex × infra_multiplier
  • annual_depreciation = среднее total_capex за последние useful_life_years
